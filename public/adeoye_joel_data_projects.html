Key policy events & analyst insights
🔴
1986 — SAP Deregulation. Price rose from ₦0.20 → ₦0.39. The first major subsidy cut under structural adjustment.
🔴
2012 — Jonathan Protests. Subsidy removal pushed price briefly to ₦141 before public outcry forced a reversal to ₦97.
🔴
2023 — "Subsidy is gone." Tinubu's inaugural declaration sent prices from ₦184 → ₦617 overnight, then ₦897 by Q4 2024.
📉
Naira correlation: Every 10% naira decline maps to ~8.3% fuel price increase — a clear import cost pass-through effect.
